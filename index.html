Coolie 😎

The 1983 Bollywood action-drama film Coolie is centered on the life of Iqbal (played by Amitabh Bachchan), who is separated from his mother, Salma (Waheeda Rehman), and brother during a devastating flood. The tragedy was orchestrated by the malicious Zafar Khan (Kader Khan). As an adult, Iqbal becomes the leader of the railway porters, or "coolies," and fights for their rights. The plot follows a classic "lost and found" narrative, as Iqbal's path eventually crosses with his long-lost family members. The story also famously includes a near-fatal on-set injury to Bachchan, which resulted in a modified ending where his character survives.

Amaran 😎

The 2024 Tamil film Amaran is a biopic celebrating the life and ultimate sacrifice of Major Mukund Varadarajan. The story is told from the perspective of his wife, Indhu Rebecca Varghese, as she prepares to receive the Ashok Chakra, India's highest peacetime gallantry award, on his behalf. The narrative chronicles their poignant college romance and marriage, showcasing their deep bond and her unwavering support for his career in the Indian Army despite family reservations. The film effectively contrasts his personal life with his professional valor, particularly detailing his deployment in Kashmir with the 44th Battalion of the Rashtriya Rifles

GOAT 😎

The recent Tamil film The Greatest of All Time (often abbreviated as GOAT) is a high-octane sci-fi action thriller starring Thalapathy Vijay in dual roles. The story follows Gandhi, a former agent of a Special Anti-Terrorism Squad, who finds his troubled past resurfacing years later when his family is once again endangered. The narrative takes a surprising turn as he confronts his own son, Jeevan, now grown and mysteriously involved in a sinister plot. Directed by Venkat Prabhu, the film utilizes de-aging technology to portray the younger version of Vijay's character, and is loaded with twists and emotional drama that keep the audience engaged. With its mix of family sentiment, humor, and spectacular action sequences, GOAT offers a fast-paced and entertaining cinematic experience for the star's fans.

Eleven 😎

This Indian crime thriller follows ACP Aravind as he investigates a disturbing series of murders where a killer forces one twin to kill the other. The initial investigation, though slow, gives way to a suspenseful second half, which is punctuated by a major plot twist. It is revealed that the killer, Aravindan, is seeking revenge for his brother's death and is driven by childhood trauma. The film explores themes of revenge, trauma, and moral ambiguity, with strong performances and a gripping climax that keeps viewers engaged until the final, shocking revelation.
